ABOUT ME

My name is Kelly Guy and I'm fully employed at a local hospital and wanted to have a side business to save for retirement. I create pieces after work, during weekends and this motivates me, knowing that my art can inspire someone. It brings a smile to my face when a customer sends me pictures either wearing by beanies or displaying art pieces in ther homes.

I started creating art pieces to increase self esteem among young African American women when a family member was being teased. We go through so much and I want to remind US that we matter, are beautiful and can accomplish anything! A customer should by from me because they SEE themselves in my art pieces, they can customize to relfect their interests, hobbies and while also making great gifts too.

My mission is to create mindful pieces of art that speak to one's soul and highlights the beauty of the African American silhouette = "Mindful Soulhettes".
